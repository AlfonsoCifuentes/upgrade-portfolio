ALFONSO CIFUENTES ALONSO

Full-Stack Web Developer, sound designer and 3D artist

Alfonso Cifuentes Alonso is a spanish Full-Stack web developer using HTML,CSS and JAVASCRIPT. He is also a sound designer with many years of experience, and a talented 3D Artist.

He was born in Madrid, and interested in computers since he was a child. Starting with MS-DOS, now he is attracted to the use of the technologies and learning many things and disciplines.

He is also into music, producing electronic music, mainly within the techno genre, and he is also a DJ

  • In 2008, he became a Sound Technician in Madrid, and worked mainly with dubbing companies such as DL Multimedia, Enixma Digital and Smile and Learn, providing also his own voice as a voice artist to a lot of projects.
  • In 2017 he also learned 3D modeling using mainly 3D Studio MAX, Zbrush and Substance Painter, and continued learning by himself using Unreal Engine,creating games for demo purposes. He also made some models for projects into Smile and Learn company, some of them for virtual reality
  • In 2020, when de pandemic came, he thought that becoming a Full-Stack developer was something that would help himself reaching a new professional level, and started learning by himself with platforms like Udemy
